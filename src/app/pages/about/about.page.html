OncDeck

What is OncDeck?

  • Mobile phone-based workflow manager for oncology care providers
  • More than an EMR, accessible from a phone or desktop computer
  • Provides easy-to-use interface allowing providers to:
    1. Enter and review clinical information for patients
    2. Share patient care details with one another
    3. Identify patients who are at greatest risk for experiencing barriers to care
    4. Review programmatic indicators to inform quality improvement efforts
